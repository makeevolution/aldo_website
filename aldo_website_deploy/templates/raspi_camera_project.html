Home Camera Security using Raspberry Pi

Concepts used: Image Processing, Internet of Things (IoT)
Applications used: Python, IFTTT, SMTP Mail Server, OpenCV, Raspberry Pi, Flask

Here a Google Home is integrated to work with a camera-equipped Raspberry Pi. When leaving the house and saying "turn on camera" to Google Home, camera will be turned on. When the camera detects motion, the camera will capture a picture and send it to a client email, notifying me that someone is in the house. Motion is detected using frame difference technique, with the reference frame being updated every 5 seconds (determined by trial and error) to ensure robustness of the system.
