About me

About

Aldo Sebastian currently works as a Technical Software Test Engineer for Sogeti Nederland. He has a MSc. in Mechanical Engineering from Delft University of Technology, specializing in High Tech Engineering. His MSc. thesis was about breaking the fundamental limitations of linear controllers (such as PID) through using a nonlinear control scheme called reset control, which is in process for publication in the 2021 IEEE/ASME International Conference on Advanced Intelligent Mechatronics (AIM).

In his free time, he likes to do weightlifting. Due to the Corona situation, he channels this hobby through calisthenics, currently working on perfecting his muscle-ups and getting towards being able to do a handstand. He also likes to program, currently during weekends attempting to make his own version of SpaceShooter using C++ and SFML.
